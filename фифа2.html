FIFA 21

FIFA 21 is the 28th computer game in the FIFA series in the genre of football simulator. The game was released on PC, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Nintendo Switch on October 9, 2020, and on the next-generation platforms (PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S) on December 3, 2020.

Changes

After years of criticism from the community, some changes have been added to the career mode, mainly the coach mode: interactive match simulation, allowing you to enter and exit the match as you go; improved training and player development; improved AI, which means more meaningful actions both in attack and defense (now players under AI control make more informed decisions in custody, selections, passes and dribbling); new transfer options (transfers become even more realistic, offering teams under the control of the user and AI new opportunities to acquire players).
