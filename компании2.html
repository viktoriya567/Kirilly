Company

Sony Corp

Sony remains the largest video game company in the world in 2020. The Japanese tech giant still gets most of its revenue from games, which totaled $ 20.3 billion, with the PlayStation 4, which came out in late 2013 and continues to be the most popular console worldwide.
However, Sony also produces many original video games for its console. The company's PlayStation Vita handheld game console also helped Sony take the top spot in the video game market.

Microsoft Corporation

The world's largest software company, is also the world's third-largest video game company. Microsoft made about $ 11 billion from Xbox Game Studios, the company's gaming arm. Of course, gaming revenue was mostly generated by Xbox One sales, but Microsoft additionally develops video games itself or owns studios that do so. A good example of this is Mojang. The Swedish video game studio, owned by Microsoft, is known for creating Minecraft, which recently became the most popular video game in history.

Take-Two Interactive Software Inc

The company owns two major publishing labels, Rockstar Games and 2K, which operate in-house game development studios. More recently, Take-Two created the Private Division label to support publications from independent developers and acquired developer Socialpoint to establish itself in the mobile gaming market. Take-Two's combined portfolio includes such franchises as BioShock, Borderlands, Grand Theft Auto, NBA 2K, and Red Dead.
